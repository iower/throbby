throbby

Don't waste time writing throbber, use the ready one!

How to use

  1. Add the throbby stylesheet to the head of your page:

    <link rel="stylesheet" href="throbby.css"></link>

  2. Create the element with one of the throbby classes:

    <div class="throbby-wheel"></div>
